Somewhere out there is a girl.
A girl whose charm loses only to the allure of her mind,
whose childish playfulness serves only to accentuate her maturity 
who loves reading in cozy comfort just as much as an arduous trek
who loves to fall asleep staring at the twinkling night sky,
and to wake up to the singing of birds at the break of dawn
who isn't afraid to get her hands dirty in the mud, 
only to have it all washed clean under the drizzling rain
Somewhere out there, there is a girl, the girl of my dreams.
