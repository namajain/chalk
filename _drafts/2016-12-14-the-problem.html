--- layout: post title: The problem date: '2016-12-14T05:25:00.004-08:00' author: naman jain tags: modified_time: '2018-10-06T03:33:19.978-07:00' thumbnail: https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-N87XRrgXuo0/WFFI1DTQI4I/AAAAAAAAU5c/6Njahz0YHsI9zSS-1Oa0TywpXmCcgdFgwCLcB/s72-c/DSCF0087.jpg blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-1234810922657092732.post-7877850718136609984 blogger_orig_url: https://legacynj.blogspot.com/2016/12/the-problem.html --- Like everyone else, I have many dreams. I want to write a novel, travel the world, make games, become famous, make lots of money, delve into psychology, start a youtube channel, write a blog, learn trading, be fearless. But I feel I haven't made significant progress towards them. I have dabbled in everything but left after encountering small roadblocks.

It isn't that I'm afraid to put in the effort or work hard. No, what I'm afraid of is the uncertainty, unknown situations. I need someone to show me the way, I don't like to go out of my comfort zone and figure stuff out, no that's annoying, and painful, instead what I do?

I play games, watch animes, read novels, indulge myself without using my brain. I banish these dreams in a corner of my mind, saying I will address them soon, but that soon becomes be too long. A similar situation arises when studying for the exams, but there is a pretty good thing about it, deadlines, they jolt me into taking action and because of that, I am able to maintain a decent enough grade.

But the problem with life goals are - there are no deadlines, no wake-up calls. If you don't do anything now, chances are, you won't do anything ever. It's ironic because I recently wrote a post about this same issue without realizing I myself am suffering from it.

But the situation isn't all that bad now. The first step to improvement is awareness. I have started understanding my flaws. Slowly but steadily I'm improving. I become better with each passing day. Soon, the world will bow before my awesomeness :p
Jokes aside, I will work on self-control, and not giving up any task until I become proficient in it, no matter how repulsive it seems.
